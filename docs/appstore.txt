Name: Pomodoor

Summery:

Pomo




Description:

Tic Toc Tic Toc .... Study time is here!!

HOW TO USE:
- Get your study materials or task ready
- Press play to start (The timer will automatically change the different zones)
    - FOCUS ZONE: 25 Minute timer
    - SHORT BREAK TIME: 5 minute timer
    - LONG BREAK TIME: 30 Minutes (after two cycles of FOCUS and SHORT BREAK)

This App does not have a lot of features because it doesnt want to take up your timer.
Our goal is to get you focusing on you and less on your phone. If you forget that you had this running, we have done our job!
